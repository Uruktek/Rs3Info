Elder Overload Potion

The apex of the overload line — a full crafting walkthrough and recipe flowchart, built tier by tier.

The elder overload combines the boosting properties of all six combat extreme potions, then upgrades twice. Each dose lifts Attack, Strength, Defence, Ranged, Magic, and Necromancy by 17% + 5 for six minutes.

How the boost behaves

Like a normal overload, the elder overload reapplies its boosts every 15 seconds, so the stat gains barely decay across the six-minute window. Once it wears off the boosts are removed entirely, and you'll get a chatbox warning shortly before that happens. An elder overload (6) is also an ingredient in the elder overload salve, which layers on prayer restoration and protection from dragonfire, wyvern breath, and poison.
